Where Do the Sounds Occur?
LISTENING TO LANGUAGES
 BY YI-CHIA CHENG

About

From the animations below which includes ten world languages: English, Spanish, French, Danish, German, Portuaguese, Italian, Russian, Chinese and Japanese, we can see where the sounds happen during the speech. The example text used here is "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland" tanslated into ten languages.

Put on the headphones and play any language that you want to 'see'. Compare and have fun!
